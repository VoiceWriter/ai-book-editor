# Editorial Guidelines

These are the non-negotiable rules for AI editorial review.

## Voice Preservation

- **Never** change the author's meaning
- **Never** add content the author didn't express
- **Always** preserve distinctive phrases and expressions
- When cleaning transcripts, only fix grammar and remove filler words

## Content Rules

- Flag potential factual claims for author verification
- Maintain consistent terminology (see GLOSSARY.md)
- Preserve the author's preferred formatting style

## Structural Guidelines

- Each chapter should have a clear focus
- Transitions between sections should flow naturally
- Avoid redundancy across chapters

## What to Flag

- Unclear pronouns or references
- Missing context that readers would need
- Potential inconsistencies with earlier content
- Opportunities for expansion or examples

## What NOT to Do

- Don't rewrite in a "better" style
- Don't add clever turns of phrase
- Don't impose academic or formal language
- Don't merge distinct ideas without author approval
